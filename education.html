A global education for the world of today and tomorrow.

Rooted in experience.

 

Dream it.

From 2007 to 2013, Symington studied at the Beijing No. 55 IB World School. During this time, he was elected president of the International Student Council, founded the school's first student-run café, reformed the school's old broadcasting system, and created its first student-led prom in the American tradition. In 2012, he represented the American student community in China and met then U.S. Secretary of State, Hillary Clinton, and former Vice Premier of China, Liu Yandong, welcoming them to the National Museum of China for the 3rd Annual U.S.-China High-level Consultation on People-to-People Exchange. He graduated valedictorian and delivered the valedictory to the class of 2013. During the ceremony, he was awarded a Certificate of Merit for his achievements and outstanding grades. His graduation made national headlines across China, being covered by major organizations such as Sina Weibo, The Red Cross Society of China, Sina Corp, and iQiyi. A video of his graduation uploaded in 2013 by Shanghai Television to YouTube amassed over 51 thousand views.

 
 

Achieve it.

From 2013 to 2018, Symington studied at the Chinese Language and Literature Department at Peking University (PKU), one of Asia's most elite and prestigious universities, long considered the Harvard and Oxford of Asia. At PKU, he was very active outside the classroom: In 2016, he played an advisory role helping the co-founders of Peking University's Fitness Association, and Peking University's Blockchain Association. In the same year, Symington was selected to serve as a teaching assistant to Professor Li Yuxin for PKU’s tennis team, where he led a class of international students to train in the winter cold. For his achievements and contributions, he was featured twice in Peking University Youth, the university's on-campus student press.

In 2019, Symington was accepted to masters programs at 15 different institutions, including the University of Chicago, University of San Francisco, King's College London, University of Sydney, University of York, Royal Holloway, SOAS London and more for his masters studies. He chose to enrol at King's College London (KCL), and begun his Masters of Science studies in the social sciences, focusing on China and Globalisation at KCL’s Lau China Institute in September 2019. Tutored by Prof. Kerry Brown FRSA, Symington graduated with merit in November 2020. Symington is also a member of KCL’s Conservative Association, Diplomacy Society, and United Nations Association.
