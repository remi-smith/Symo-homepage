Col. Symington W. Smith

FRAS FRSA MCIL CL

Col. Symington W. Smith FRAS FRSA MCIL CL is an award-winning global affairs expert and youth advocate. As the youngest vice president of the Beijing Chamber of Commerce in Thailand, marketing director of Inertia Ventures in New York, and a board member of the Eastern Commerce Group, his mission is to inspire and show the world that with action, young people can and do change the world.
